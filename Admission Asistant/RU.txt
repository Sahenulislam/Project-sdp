
							



									University of Rajshahi
								   --------------------------------

				   The University of Rajshahi, also known as Rajshahi University or RU. The university's 60 departments
 				   are organised into ten faculties. Rajshahi University is in a 305-hectare (753-acre) campus in Motihar, 
				   3 kilometres (2 mi) from the Rajshahi city centre.
